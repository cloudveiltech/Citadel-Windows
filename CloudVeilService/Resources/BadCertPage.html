SSL Certificate Error

{{friendly_url_text}} does not have a valid SSL certificate.

What can I do to fix this?

Open CloudVeil for Windows, go to 'Advanced' and click 'view certificate errors'. Then select the entry with host '{{host}}' and click 'trust certificate'

Why did this happen?

You tried to access a site that CloudVeil detected as not having a proper certificate. This might mean that the site is compromised. Proceed with caution.
